Math circles have been a part of my life for as long as I can remember; I have been attending the UCLA Math Circle for the past 10 years, where I immersed myself in creative problem solving. Later, I wanted to share my experiences with others, so I started a UCLA Math Circle Satellite Program at my local elementary school in 7th grade called the Highland Oaks Math Circle.

During the summer of 2020, I started the Pascal Math Circle (for APTA) and the Dana Math Circle (for my local middle school) to continue sharing my knowledge. Most recently, I started a Math Kangaroo Training Camp for APTA.
